  • Podcast

A podcast is a digital audio file that is available online for a computer or portable device. Podcasts are typically available in a series, and can be subscribed to so that new episodes are automatically available for the listener's device. Many podcasts are free to listen to and cover a wide range of topics, from news and politics to comedy and music. Some podcasts are produced by professional media organizations, while others are created by individuals or small groups. Podcasts can be listened to on a variety of devices, including smartphones, tablets, and computers, and can be streamed or downloaded for offline listening.
